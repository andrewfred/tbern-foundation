BIOGRAPHY OF ANTHONY DOVI

  • HOME
  • OUR TEAM

Anthony Bernard Dovi is the founder and head of Tbern Foundation.

He graduated from the University of Ghana, Legon with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Political Science and Information Studies in 2008. He subsequently obtained a Post Graduate Certificate in Business Administration from the Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration(GIMPA).

In 2018 he graduated from the Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration(GIMPA) with a Bachelor of Laws, LLB (First Class Honors).His subjects of interest are Environmental Law, Company Law and Tort Law.Born in Keta, Anthony spent his boyhood exploring the coastal communities, beaches and fields around the coast. Due the the increased rate in environmental degradation, he is now an environmental advocate helping to preserve, conserve and protect the environment.
