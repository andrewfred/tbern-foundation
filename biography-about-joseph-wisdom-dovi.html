BIOGRAPHY OF JOSEPH WISDOM DOVI

  • HOME
  • OUR TEAM

Joseph is an innovative business executive with multi spectral expertise in business, enterpreneurship, fashion, international trade and social innovativs.

He holds a High National Diploma in Marketing from Ho Technical University and he is currently pursuing Bsc Marketing Management at Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration(GIMPA).

He is the Founder & CEO of Ghana Fashion Marketing Group a Ghanaian private owned limited company which focuses it's primary activity on marketing and promoting Ghana fashion, arts, crafts, style, lifestyle and culture to global competitive world market and aims at providing business development solutions and capacity building services for fashion startups, small and medium fashion business/enterprises in Ghana.

Joseph is also passionate about preservation of the natural environment and have interest in waste management, protectiom of water bodies and very attentive projects such as tree planting, beach cleanliness etc.
